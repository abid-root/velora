Process

A process built on precision and partnership.

Seven clear stages help buyers move from brief to delivery with better control.

Step 01

Requirement Analysis

We review brief, quality target, quantity, fabric requirements, timeline and commercial expectations.

Step 02

Design & Development

We support sketches, tech packs, samples, measurements and development communication.

Step 03

Sourcing & Planning

Fabric, trims, factory selection, costing and production planning are coordinated early.

Step 04

Production

Order execution is followed through selected factory partners with clear updates.

Step 05

Quality Control

Inspection checkpoints help identify issues early and maintain product consistency.

Step 06

Packing & Logistics

Packing instructions, cartons, shipment documents and logistics are prepared carefully.

Step 07

Delivery & Aftercare

Final delivery follow-up, buyer communication and post-shipment coordination.
