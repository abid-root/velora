FAQs

Questions buyers usually ask before starting.

Quick answers about sourcing, categories, compliance, forms, and website setup.

What kind of buyers does Velora work with?

Velora is designed for fashion brands, importers, sourcing teams, retail groups, and private-label buyers looking for apparel production and supply chain support.

Does Velora own factories?

Velora works through selected production partners and associated factories. The website is structured as a sourcing and coordination company, not a single factory-only site.

Can you handle sampling and tech-pack development?

Yes. The service structure includes design interpretation, tech-pack review, sample follow-up, fabric and trim coordination, and fit development support.

What categories can be sourced?

Core categories include woven apparel, knitwear, denim, outerwear, activewear, kidswear, home textiles, accessories, sweaters, T-shirts, workwear, and jackets.

Can certificates be shown on the website?

Only verified certificates from the company or partner factories should be shown. The site includes a compliance area, but the final documents must be checked before publishing.

Is the contact form connected to a backend?

No. This version is a static website. The form validates input and opens an email draft. A backend or form service can be added later.

Can this site be hosted without a custom server?

Yes. It can be hosted as a static website on Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages, or cPanel hosting.
