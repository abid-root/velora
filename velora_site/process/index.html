Sourcing process

A structured path from buyer brief to aftercare.

Every stage is designed to improve communication, reduce risk, and keep production aligned with buyer expectations.

01

Requirement Analysis

We review the buyer’s product brief, target price, quantity, fabric needs, quality expectations, and timeline.

02

Design & Development

We support sketches, tech packs, samples, measurements, trims, and development communication.

03

Sourcing & Planning

We coordinate fabric, trims, accessories, suitable factory selection, costing, and production planning.

04

Production

We follow up with factory partners to support order execution, timeline control, and production visibility.

05

Quality Control

We support inspection checkpoints to help identify issues early and maintain product consistency.

06

Packing & Logistics

We assist with packing instructions, carton preparation, shipment documents, and logistics coordination.

07

Delivery & Aftercare

We support final delivery follow-up, buyer communication, and post-shipment coordination.
