FAQs

Questions buyers ask before starting.

Clear answers for apparel sourcing, production coordination and documentation.

What does Velora do?

Velora supports apparel sourcing, product development, production coordination, quality assurance and logistics follow-up for international buyers.

Do you own factories?

Velora works with selected factory partners and helps coordinate buyer requirements with appropriate production capacity.

Can you work from a tech pack?

Yes. Tech packs, reference samples, images and measurement sheets help us review development needs and next steps.

Which categories do you support?

Woven, knitwear, denim, outerwear, activewear, kidswear, home textiles, accessories, sweaters, T-shirts, workwear and jackets.

Can you support compliance documents?

Compliance documents should be verified by the company or partner factories before being presented to buyers.

How do buyers start?

Send a product brief, category, target quantity, delivery timeline, fabric direction and any sample references through the quote page.
